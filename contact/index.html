About Me

Mission Statement

As a designer, what matters most to me while designing is accessibility, ethical design, and creating a design that evokes political or social questions. More specifically, values-driven design makes me more creative and passionate. If I am given a design task with an open subject, I often choose a social issue that I am interested in at that time. Therefore, I want to create a design that arouses their interest in the issue.
