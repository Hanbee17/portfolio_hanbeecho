Project 3

Type: Brand Identity

In this project, students will learn how to design the intention and brand for a fictional non-profit organization in groups. They will explore the relationship between form, content, and context by developing an identity, refining their messaging, and applying it across print and digital media. The project aims to teach students how to build research, work collaboratively in a group, and use visual communication effectively.
