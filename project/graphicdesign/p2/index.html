Sun, Moon and Five Peaks

Type: Poster

The goal of this poster is for students to visually represent an abstract idea, using metaphors and storytelling to offer clarity and understanding to viewers. Emphasis is on exploring diverse processes, techniques, and methods to expand visual skill sets. The project requires students to create a poster with a calendar for the 2023 year, with the calendar being less than a quarter of the poster and not in a conventional format. The poster should contain the student’s visualization of their experience with the “80th Birthday Party” video as the first level of information, and the calendar as the second level. The final mock-up will be presented in both physical and digital formats.
