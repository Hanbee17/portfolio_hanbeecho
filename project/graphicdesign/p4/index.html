This fruit tea package design embodies a contemporary and inviting atmosphere through the utilization of vector graphics and a pastel color scheme. The design seeks to captivate with its modern aesthetics while conveying a sense of warmth and friendliness, aligning seamlessly with the essence of the fruit tea within.
