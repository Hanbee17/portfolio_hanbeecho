Tough As Whole Grains

Type: A poster/booklet

Software Used:

This booklet aims to introduce the contemporary Japanese architect, tkd Tadao, and his architectural contributions.
Throughout its design, my goal was to convey my deep admiration for tkd's architectural style and ethos.

In paying homage to tkd’s work, I carefully crafted the design elements to resonate with his architectural imagery. For instance, the cover art mirrors the essence of tkd’s initial creation, the Row House, showcasing its minimalist design and affinity for exposed concrete. The chosen color palette of grey, black, and white was purposeful, intending to underscore tkd’s adept use of light within his designs.
