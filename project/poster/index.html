Poster Design

The Path of Exploration

Type: Informational Poster

This is an informational poster that has a visual representation of an object;
a suitcase and my personal story related to it.

This project is designed to deliver a graphical representation of my suitcase and my personal history with it. At the bottom, there’s a timeline of the places I have lived ever since I studied abroad since my age of fifteen years old. I always used the suitcase in the poster to pack my things as I moved from South Korea to Thailand, Ireland, Belgium and United States. I wanted to engage with the viewer by illustrating this informational poster to communicate a better context and understanding.
