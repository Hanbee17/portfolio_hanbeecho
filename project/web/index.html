Interactive Website

The Names

Type: An Interactive Website

The Names project is to commemorate the deceased who died as a result of man-made disasters.

I have always considered myself a commercial designer rather than an artistic designer. However, this project taught me how socially interested I am in political issues and how much I wanted to use my design skills to express that. This project is informed by my ongoing question: Why should people move on from loss? For this project, I made an interactive website exploring the mass death caused by a ferry ship incident in South Korea, the Sewol Ferry Tragedy. When I was brainstorming for this project, it was the eighth anniversary of the Sewol Ferry Tragedy, and there was a lot of criticism of the bereaved families who can’t move on from the tragedy.
