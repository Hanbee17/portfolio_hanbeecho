MyGenBio Inc.

MyGenBio is a California-based health tech company specializing in AI-driven supplements and digital wellness solutions. Their core innovation, BNO supplements, enhances nitric oxide production using natural ingredients to support cardiovascular and cognitive health. Through their MYGENBIO Dashboard App in development, they will offer personalized health tracking and recommendations to promote lifelong well-being.

At MyGenBio, I served as the Lead Designer responsible for both digital and print design initiatives.

My work involved UI/UX design in Figma, branding and packaging development (including bottle and sachet labels), and marketing collateral such as brochures, social media graphics, and banners.

I collaborated with cross-department teams to ensure visual consistency and timely delivery of assets, often iterating on feedback to refine the company’s design language. My contributions supported the launch of multiple campaigns and products, enhancing both user experience and brand visibility.

Website Design (Corporate & E-commerce)

I led the design of both the corporate website and e-commerce platform. The split-site strategy allowed us to communicate MyGenBio’s scientific foundation while optimizing the product sales experience through Shopify.

Problem

MyGenBio lacked an integrated digital presence that could communicate both its science-backed brand values and effectively convert interest into product sales. The original single-site structure was not scalable and diluted both scientific credibility and user shopping experience.

Goal

Design two complementary web experiences:

- A corporate site (coded with front-end development) to present the company’s vision, science, and credibility.

- A Shopify e-commerce site to streamline product discovery and drive supplement sales.

Solution

I led the full design direction of both sites:

- For the corporate site, I designed an elegant and information-driven layout emphasizing scientific legitimacy, product research, and company story. Using modular UI components and a strong grid system, I collaborated with developers to ensure the front-end code translated my designs pixel-perfectly.

Visit the corporate site

- For Shopify site, I developed a product-focused, conversion-optimized layout with custom-designed sections, curated imagery, and thoughtful product categorization. I adapted brand visuals across templates to ensure consistency while prioritizing clarity and ease of use for customers.

Visit the Shopfify site

Result

The separation of the two platforms allowed each to excel in its purpose—education and conversion. The corporate site enhanced investor and partner trust by clearly showcasing innovation and research, while Shopify site improved customer engagement and sales performance. Together, they delivered a cohesive yet functional digital ecosystem that reflects MyGenBio’s dual identity as a science-first and consumer-focused brand.

App Design (Figma)

I designed the MyGenBio mobile app from scratch, taking full ownership of the UX/UI process as the sole designer. The app serves as a key future tool to help users manage their personalized health data and supplement plans. While development is still in progress, the design system is fully realized and handed off to engineers.

Problem

MyGenBio needed a personalized health app to present complex biological data in a user-friendly format. With no existing app and no design support, I had to build the experience from the ground up—alone.

Goal

Design a mobile dashboard app that is intuitive, data-driven, and accessible to a wide demographic, including users unfamiliar with health tracking tools.

Solution

Using Figma, I led the design of a responsive app UI that simplifies health data visualization. I crafted wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes with a clean layout, modular components, and interactive elements to ensure user clarity and accessibility.

App in Developement

Result

The final app design improved user engagement by offering a visually clear health summary and intuitive navigation. It laid the foundation for the development team and helped MyGenBio secure investor interest by showcasing innovation and usability.
