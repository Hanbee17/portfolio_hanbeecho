MyGenBio Inc.

MyGenBio is a California-based health tech company specializing in AI-driven supplements and digital wellness solutions. Their core innovation, BNO supplements, enhances nitric oxide production using natural ingredients to support cardiovascular and cognitive health. Through their MYGENBIO Dashboard App, they offer personalized health tracking and recommendations to promote lifelong well-being.

At MyGenBio, I served as the Lead Designer responsible for both digital and print design initiatives.

My work involved UI/UX design in Figma, branding and packaging development (including bottle and sachet labels), and marketing collateral such as brochures, social media graphics, and banners.

My contributions supported the launch of multiple campaigns and products, enhancing both user experience and brand visibility.
